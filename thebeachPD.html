The Beach

A 2.5D shooting platformer game where you play as a soldier trying to survive the beach. This was a personal project to explore game mechanics in Unity.

Project Overview

The Beach is a 2.5D shooting platformer game I developed as a fun, personal project to experiment with Unity's game mechanics. The game focuses on intense gameplay, where players must survive waves of enemies while navigating a challenging beach environment. This project allowed me to explore coding, design, and prototyping techniques while creating a polished, playable game.

Development Process

To bring the game to life, I followed these steps:

  • Paper Prototyping: Created a simple paper prototype to map out the gameplay mechanics and level design.
  • Game Mechanics: Coded core mechanics such as player movement, shooting, and enemy AI in Unity.
  • Level Design: Designed the beach environment, adding interactive elements to challenge and engage players.
  • Visual Effects: Incorporated animations, particle effects, and sound to enhance the player experience.
